Local Dishes



Eskisehir is rich in traditional, very tasteful and various foods. Locals and visitors can eat and in many restaurants, caffe bars or buy the fast food at the street. Visitors are amazed by the special taste of traditional food. In Eskisehir, you can eat piyaz, köfte, cibörek with ayran, pide, kebab, doner, spicy food and olive. You can also find a various sweet food, desserts and cakes such are baklava, sekerpare tatli,taninmiº helvaci, Incir Waffle and many others. You can enjoy in turkish tee and turkish coffe too.
