Historical Places

Eskisehir is full of historically important monuments and is perhaps the best place to witness history unfold. The wonderful monuments and the rich history attached with the destination make Eskisehir one of top historical destinations in the world. Eskisehir is a very historically important place and thus sees a lot of tourists every year. Here are five, the most visited places in Eskisehir.

Sazova Park

Adress:SazovaMh. Tepebasi/ Eskisehir, Turkey

Timing: 10:00AM - 05:00PM

...Park, Castle, Food and Drinks, Theatre, Lake, Train Ride...

A green area, excellent way to spend an afternoon, especially with family.It is relaxing place among green grass which also offers light meal and drink. The visitors can explore the castle and ship and Science center. Sazova is good place for both, adults and children.

Yazilikaya

Adress: Merkez, YazilikayaKoyu, Han, Eskisehir, Turkey

Timing: 10:00AM - 06:00PM

Popularly known as the Tomb of King Midas, Yazilikaya is a favourite attraction among tourists and locals alike. Excavated by the French Archaeological Institute immediately after the Second World War, the place is located 66 kilometres south of Eskisehir. Visitors can see Monument with Phrygian inscriptions, Temple front with acroteria faced with terra cotta, Kirkgoz cave (Forty eye cave).The whole excavated site takes almost 2 hours to explore. A should visit it if you love history archaeology.

Luleltasi Muzesi

Adress:IkiEylul Cad.Odunpazari, Eskisehir, Turkey

Timing: 10:00AM - 05:00PM

Meerschaum, better known as Sepiolite, is a mineral which is often used to make elaborate smoke pipes. Located in the old part of the city, the building sits amidst the charm. At the museum you will find sepioloite pipes which will make you wonder if they are in fact made from marble! Scroll through an array of them which showcase detailing like never before.If you do not mind spending, gift the pipes to your friends!

Archaeological Museum

Adress:Ataturk Bulv. No:64 | Eskisehir Merkez,Eskisehir, Turkey

Timing: 09:00AM - 07:00PM

The Archaeological Museum in Eskisehir is truly an experience in itself. The museum incorporates bronze artifacts, pots, earthenware, metallic articles and more. The highlights include: Stimulated chariot ride, Hologram displays, Virtual King's Tomb Tour. The showcased pieces are well translated while the place itself is well arranged, making a quick scan for history lovers and tourists!

Odunpazari
Adress:Eskisehir, Turkey

Timing: 24-hrs

If you want to walk back in time in Turkey and see it reflected in the houses which adorn the old city, walk through the Odunpazari- the old part of the city! Small streets lined with colourful houses give the place an old-world charm which can only be experienced by exploring its nooks and crannies. A must if you love to explore unique spaces of a city. You want to carry a camera for this one!
